Tuna Fish Sandwich

A yummy fish sandwich, that's easy to make.

One serving

Ingredients

  • Can of tuna
  • Two slices of bread
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Empty the can of tuna into a bowl.
  2. Dallop a spoonfull or two of mayonnaise into the bowl and mix.
  3. Spread on the bread.
  4. Enjoy.
