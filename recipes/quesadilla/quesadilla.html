A simple quesadilla

This recipe is dead simple and is a staple at my home. If you want to make it nicer, feel free to add any ingredients you'd like in step 4.

Makes one serving.

Ingredients

  • Two tortillas
  • Grated Cheese
  • A scoop each of salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

Instructions

  1. Put the first tortilla in a large shallow pan, and turn the stove on medium until underside is golden or light brown.
  2. Set aside the frist tortilla (having only cooked one side), and do the same thing to the second tortilla.
  3. Flip the second tortilla and lower the heat.
  4. Put cheese onto the second tortilla, then place the first tortilla on top, making sure to put the golden or brown side down.
  5. Keep on heat until the cheese has melted enough to flip the quesadilla without making a mess.
  6. Flip the quesadilla, and keep it on heat long enough to melt the rest of the chease and gild the back of the tortilla.
  7. Place finished quesadilla on a plate, slice it into triangles, and scoop the salsa, sour cream, and guacamole onto it.
  8. Enjoy.
